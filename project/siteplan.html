Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to develop a website for an agricultural organization that specializes in offering a range of services including consultancy, farm setup, equipment leasing, and investment opportunities different corps farming like cassava, vegetables, groundnut, and plantain farming to help people who has interest in farming but do not have the luxury of time to participate in it. It will also help farmer to manage and maximize their farm output through our consultancy services and equipment leasing. The website will provide detailed information about these services, and allow visitors to subscribe to any of the services of their interest.

Audience

The audience of this website are farmers who needs our consultancy expertise to improve their productivities. It will enable other farms do not have enough equipements like tractor to lease our equipment at an affordable costs. The site is also for working class people who is interested in farming but do not have the luxury of time to physically participate in farming but wish to invest in our options of crop farming with return of their investment shared to them when the time of maturity reach.
